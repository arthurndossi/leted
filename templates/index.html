About us {#

Take a quick tour of about us!

#}

LETED company Ltd exists to bring outstanding value to our partners and importantly our customers. We do this by combining our technology expertise with advanced services in the design, planning and deployment of sophisticated, affordable technological solutions to meet and exceed in the most demanding needs of modern business.

Apart from leading in professional services engagement with our clients, we also distribute unique technology in Information Technology, construction and logistic industry. By identifying the correct partners, vendors and management companies, LETED company Ltd provides a full spectrum enablement to new and current companies in the market.

Read More

The management of LETED company Ltd are all well established and seasoned certified professionals with more than 9 years collective experience having worked in companies like National Internet Data Center, Huawei, Samsung, CWS and CISCO to name a few.

LETED company Ltd management are well respected individuals in the market with a successful track record across the business spectrum and pride themselves on their product selection, partnership models, after sales service an ability to deliver strategic solutions, value for business and importantly as a key management services broker to it's customers.

Our services

Services We Provide

Building Materials

Are you building or plan to build soon but need assistance in purchasing building materials reliably? Here we are, anytime anyplace we will deliver them to you at your convenience. Contact us in the form below now!

Web Designing & Development

Regardless you are searching to develop a professional website from scratch or need to redesign, upgrade or scale up your existing web assets, our team will help you implement your ideas. And since we stick to proactive management, you should expect to get even a better system than you've imagined.

Web Hosting

Fast, secure and reliable web hosting for your website. We offer different types of web hosting services as per the needs of your project. Contact us for further consultation.

Mobile App Development

Progressive businesses are investing in apps to generate more sales from mobile users. Our dedicated mobile app developers will guide you at every step and create a user experience-based mobile app that will help you maximize engagement on mobile.

Cyber security

Having experience from top class companies in and out of Tanzania, our team is well equipped in protecting your critical systems and sensitive information from digital attacks. We offer the following services, Information Security Consulting, Penetration Testing, Cyber Security Training and Certification, Information System (IS), Audit System Security Plan Review and Business Continuity Training.

IT Hardware & Peripherals Supply

We supply, deliver, configure and implement various ICT equipment comprising hardware, software and other ICT related supplies and peripherals.
